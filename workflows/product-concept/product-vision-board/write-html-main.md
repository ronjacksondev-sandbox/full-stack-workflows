## Write `section` elements inside `main` element

<section role="region">
  <h2>Vision</h2>
  <p>Help people understand how <em>time</em> and <em>money</em> work together.</p>

<section role="region">
  <h2>Target group</h2>

  </ul>

<section role="region">
  <h2>Product</h2>

  <p>Shared categories connect hours and dollars</p>

</section>

<section role="region">

    <dd>Find engaged early users</dd>

    <dt>Upgrade</dt>
    <dd>Introduce premium features</dd>
