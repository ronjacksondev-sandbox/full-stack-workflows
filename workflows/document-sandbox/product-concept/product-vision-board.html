Duo Budget – Product Vision Board

Vision

Help people understand how their time and money work together so they can make clearer, more confident decisions.

Target group

  • Individuals who want clarity without complexity
  • People who think in terms of both time and money
  • Users frustrated by juggling multiple tracking tools

Needs

  • A unified way to track time and money together
  • A simple structure that reduces friction and confusion
  • Clear insights that reveal patterns and habits

Product

Duo Budget is a minimal personal planning tool that uses shared categories to connect hours and dollars. Users log entries, view totals, and see their resources in one unified dashboard.

Business goals

  • Validate demand for a unified time‑and‑money model
  • Grow a base of engaged early users
  • Introduce premium features that support long‑term sustainability
