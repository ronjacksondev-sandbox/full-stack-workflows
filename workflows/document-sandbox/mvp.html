Duo Budget – MVP

Problem

People track time and money in separate tools, making it hard to see how their resources connect. Without a unified view, planning and decision‑making become guesswork.

Audience

  • People who want clarity without complexity
  • Planners who think in terms of both time and money
  • Users frustrated by juggling multiple tracking tools

Value proposition

Duo Budget unifies time and money through shared categories, giving users a simple, clear view of where their resources go.

Core user flow

  1. User creates an account and signs in.
  2. User reviews the shared category list to understand the unified structure.
  3. User logs a time entry using one of the categories.
  4. User logs a money entry using the same category.
  5. User views category totals to see time and money combined.
  6. User opens the dashboard to understand their overall resource picture.

Out of scope

  • Bank account syncing or automated financial imports
  • Calendar integrations or automated time tracking
  • AI‑driven forecasting beyond simple projections
  • Team or multi‑user collaboration features

Success criteria

User login capability
Users can create an account and return to saved data without assistance.
Shared categories
Users understand the category system without explanation.
Log time entries
Users can record a time entry within 30 seconds of onboarding.
Log money entries
Users can record a money entry within 30 seconds of onboarding.
Category totals
Users can identify at least one insight about their habits after first use.
Unified dashboard (basic totals)
Users understand the dashboard at a glance without guidance.
Yearly projections (basic)
Users can interpret projected totals without needing documentation.
Monthly summary view
Users can compare months and spot changes in under 10 seconds.
Export data (CSV)
Users can export their data and open it successfully in another tool.
Advanced projections
Users can adjust variables and immediately see meaningful changes.
Insights / recommendations
Users receive at least one actionable suggestion that feels relevant.
